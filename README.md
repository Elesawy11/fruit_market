# Fruit Market App🍇
## Welcome to fruit market 🖐🖐
## The App Provid Various types of fruits, vegetables and Dry Fruit .🔥🔥
## The App Provid show product in details, make product in your favorite, or in your cart, buy product in easy way,change your account features💪💪
## Used Firebase as data base 
## used FireStore To Store Product 
## used FireStoreage to store image of product
## used Google Auth to access account.
### State management of app is Bloc(Cubit).
### get_it package as Service Locator
### flutter_native_splash to implemnet Splash Screen for app
### flutter_screenutil to make app responsive
### go_router to control Navigation in app
### json_annotation, json_serializable, build_runner to make advanced Model
